Observations:
- zscore GAINED slightly (+0.01) — the removed CKD/DCM annotations
  were wrong pathways that zscore was ALSO getting wrong. Removing
  them doesn't change zscore much because it was near chance on those.

  annotations. Under pre-correction, UCell got DCM "right" 44.5% of
  the time on 5 pathways. Now with only TGF-beta, it gets 75% but
  the reduced denominator changes the average.
- AUCell also LOST (-0.03) — same DCM effect.
